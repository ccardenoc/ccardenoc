Guest Speaker Event

Universidad de Cartagena

Navigating Taxes, Contracts, Labor Safety, and Other Entrepreneurial Challenges

I had the honor of returning to my alma mater, the Universidad de Cartagena, as a guest speaker for an alumni event hosted by the Faculty of Engineering. This session focused on breaking down the most critical challenges that early-stage entrepreneurs and tech professionals face.

Key Topics Covered

  • How to make sense of tax systems and avoid costly mistakes.
  • Best practices for employment contracts and freelance agreements.
  • Basics of labor safety compliance in Colombia.
  • The importance of having a strategic financial and legal foundation before scaling.

Presenting key entrepreneurial strategies to the audience

With the Dean and Program Directors of the Faculty of Engineering

Salón Cruz Pombo filled with engineering students and faculty

This talk was more than a lecture—it was a personal return to the place where my career began. I shared lessons from my journey, from building scrappy MVPs to leading multi-million dollar B2B SaaS platforms as a Senior Product Manager.

"Understanding systems like taxes and labor laws isn't just a legal formality—it's a competitive advantage."
